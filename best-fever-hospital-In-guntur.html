Typically, a normal fever in a body is between 99-101 degrees Fahrenheit. Generally, a fever subsides within 3 days with the common medicines, but if you have a fever ranging more than 102 degrees, and not subsiding, it is definitely advised to see a doctor and start immediate medication.
